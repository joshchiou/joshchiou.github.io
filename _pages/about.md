I am a Senior Advisor at [Lilly](https://www.lilly.com) working on proteomics for obesity clinical trials. My research focuses on translating large-scale proteomic data into mechanistic and biomarker insights to inform clinical strategy.

Previously, I was a Senior Principal Computational Geneticist in the [Internal Medicine Research Unit](https://www.pfizer.com/science/focus-areas/internal-medicine) at [Pfizer](https://www.pfizer.com), where I partnered with biologists to propose novel targets for cardiovascular and renal diseases, led cloud-native infrastructure projects for statistical genetics, and contributed to pre-competitive consortiums such as the [UK Biobank Pharma Proteomics Project](https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/projects/large-scale-proteomic-profiling-to-facilitate-genetics-guided-drug-discovery-and-precision-medicine-the-uk-biobank-pharma-proteomics-project-ukb-ppp/).

I completed my PhD in [Biomedical Sciences](https://biomedsci.ucsd.edu/) at [UC San Diego](https://ucsd.edu/) in the [Gaulton Lab](https://gaultonlab.org/), where I used single-cell epigenomics to study the regulatory landscape of the pancreas and its relevance to the genetic risk of type 1 and type 2 diabetes.
